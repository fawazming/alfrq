About Al-Furqān Co. Ltd.

Trusted Hajj & Umrah services — care, integrity and seamless organisation.

Al-Furqān Co. Ltd. is dedicated to serving the Guests of Allah with thoughtfully organised pilgrimage journeys. From visa processing and flights to comfortable accommodation and on-ground support in Makkah and Madinah, our experienced team and trusted partners ensure a peaceful, worry-free pilgrimage.

We combine spiritual guidance with professional logistics so your focus remains on worship and reflection. Transparency, safety and sincere hospitality guide every step of our service.
